We are building a smart application to streamline the process of reporting appliance issues in office classrooms. Here's what the app will offer:

  • Employees can easily report faulty appliances by scanning a QR code displayed outside each classroom.
  • The app will display a virtual classroom layout, similar to seat selection in RedBus, where users can identify and highlight faulty appliances such as fans, lights, or projectors.
  • Admins will have a dedicated page to view and manage these reports efficiently.
  • A comprehensive history of issues will be stored in the database for tracking and analysis.

Our goal is to simplify maintenance reporting and improve office productivity through technology.
